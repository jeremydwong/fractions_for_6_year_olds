# intro

Hi Emery! Daddy made you a little playground about **fractions**. We get to cut things up, colour them in, and press buttons. Let's play and learn at the same time!

So... what is a fraction? A fraction is just a way to talk about **part of a whole thing**.

Imagine we have one yummy cookie, and we want to share it *fairly*. So we cut it into **equal** pieces — every piece exactly the same size. That's the big rule: **the pieces have to be fair, all the same size.**

A fraction is written with two numbers, one on top of the other:

- The **bottom** number says: *how many equal pieces we cut the whole thing into.*
- The **top** number says: *how many of those pieces we are talking about.*

So `1/2` means "cut it into **2** fair pieces, and take **1** of them." That's a half! And `3/4` means "cut it into **4** fair pieces, and take **3** of them."
